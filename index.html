My work.

Aco mer


ACO MER is a recipe app that filters its recipes based on the ingredients you have and your skill as a cook. It was a project for the class "Diseño de productos e innovación en TI" from the Universidad de los Andes.

Ratscape


Ratscape was a game developed for a gamejam hosted at the Universidad de los Andes, where teams of up to 5 people had to code a game on a given topic in under 24 hours. It was made using the Unity engine.
